# Billing

Learn about Spotzy.ai's billing system and how to manage your payments.

## Payment Methods

Spotzy.ai accepts:

- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- Debit cards
- ACH transfers (for enterprise plans)

## Billing Cycle

- Billing occurs monthly on your signup anniversary date
- Usage is calculated based on minutes and features used
- Invoices are sent via email

## Managing Your Subscription

### Viewing Your Current Plan

1. Go to Settings > Billing
2. View your current plan and usage
3. See upcoming charges

### Upgrading or Downgrading

You can change your plan at any time:

1. Navigate to Settings > Billing
2. Click "Change Plan"
3. Select your new plan
4. Confirm the change

### Cancellation

To cancel your subscription:

1. Go to Settings > Billing
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Provide feedback (optional)
4. Confirm cancellation
