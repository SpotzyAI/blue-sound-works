# Key Terminology

## AI Assistant
A configured voice agent with specific instructions and personality.

## Campaign
A scheduled series of automated outbound calls.

## Lead
A contact record in your database.

## System Prompt
Instructions that define how your AI assistant behaves.

## Voice Model
The synthetic voice used by your assistant.

## Webhook
Real-time notifications sent to your systems during calls.
